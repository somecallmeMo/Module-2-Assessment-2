Our Mall

Founded in 2142, Pine City Mall started as unique plan to combine modern luxury shopping and the tranquility of a our city's beloved pine forest.

At Pine City Mall we have sourced various high-end luxury brands from all around the world. Thanks to our founder, Muhammad Haffejee, we have labels who employ and support locals from their respective countries and create oppurtinies for people to build lives for themselves and thier families.

We pride ourselves in providing South Africans with and enviroment that is refreshing and promotes quality, tranquility, and safety, that is guranteed to give all our customers the peace of mind to let their hair down and safely shop to thier hearts desires.
